About

In this section, you can read about the new things I'm working on.

Like many others, I too work on too many things at once, and unfortunately most of these ideas will never see the light of day.

But some likely will, and you can read about these projects below.

Thing one

Improved Stone grinder

The Stone grinder Medium is just the first of its kind. As we speak, I am working on new and improved designs, to get as much as possible out of this idea.

I want the future models to be:

Bigger, so you can grind more herb at once.

Easier to use, for an even better experience

Smaller, making them even more convenient

Fancier, simply to make grinding a joy.

Thing two

Stainless steel grinder

While Stone grinder is unique and does its job well, I still want to take this design and make it work with stainless steel.

Nothing concrete to show at this moment, but rest assured I'll come up with something sooner or later.

Thing three

New and wierd Egg cup designs

You may not agree with me, but I am a firm believer that the egg cups of this world are simply too boring. Sure, there are some interesting designs out there, but I want to combine wierd with interesting and useful, just for the sake of it.

So far I've only managed to come up with this Square egg cup, but rest assured I'm not done. Something unique awaits somewhere in the future.
